Dusit Thani Himalayan Resort

The Dusit Thani Himalayan Resort in Dhulikhel, Nepal, is a luxurious escape nestled in the tranquil hills with sweeping views of the majestic Himalayas. Opened in 2023, the resort offers a unique blend of Thai-inspired elegance and modern comfort. It features 72 well-appointed accommodations, including deluxe rooms, spacious junior suites, and expansive villas with private kitchenettes and dining areas. Each unit is thoughtfully equipped with amenities such as Nespresso machines, rain showers, and Devarana wellness products. Guests can unwind at the 550 sq. m. spa, swim in the outdoor pool overlooking the mountains, or enjoy a workout in the state-of-the-art fitness center. Dining options include a refined restaurant serving Chinese cuisine and a cozy bar. With exceptional service, pristine surroundings, and a serene ambiance, the Dusit Thani Himalayan Resort has earned glowing reviews, making it an ideal retreat for relaxation and rejuvenation.
